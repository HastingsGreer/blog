Recently, I found myself wanting to comment on my friend Brandon's blog. Alas, none of our websites have comments sections! For this case, I was able to just whack my comment on to his page via a github pull request, but this doesn't scale- among other issues, I had to text him first to get him to turn on my ability to make pull requests. Anyways, clearly I need to add a comments section here, and hopefully I can do a good enough job that others in the ring can emulate.
